Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a type of Italian pasta which consists of flat sheets stacked on top of each other with ground meat, cheese and vegetables as fillings in the middle.

This dish is perhaps most known for being the beloved cartoon cat Garfield's favorite food. But who's surprised? Lasagna is quite the delicacy!

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Beef
  • Italian Sausage(optional)
  • Cheese
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook sausage, beef, onions and garlic
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets in a pot with boiling water
    3. >
  3. Lay out the sheets
  4. Spread the cooked meat with tomato paste and cheese onto the lasagna sheets
  5. Season the meat with garlic and pepper
  6. Cover the whole thing with foil
  7. Repeat the whole process from start to finish 2 more times for a well made lasagna.
