Vindaloo

Description:

Vindaloo, which was originally a Portuguese recipe, means "meat in wine, vinegar and garlic" and is now a popular traditional Goan recipe.

In Portuguese, the dish name goes as "Carne de vinha a'alhos", where meat, usually pork was marinated in wine and garlic. The Indian version of it is to marinate the meat in vinegar, chillies, ginger, garlic and sugar, followed by spices.

Ingredients:

  • Lamb
  • Curry Leaves
  • Chilies
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Vinegar

Steps:

  1. First add all the ingredients for the past into a blender and blend
  2. Add the blended paste to the meat along with salt and sugar
  3. Leave the mixture to marinate for at least 2 hours
  4. Put into a pressure cooker with heated oil and cook for at least 30 mins to an hour while stirring the mixture
  5. When the meat becomes tender, garnish with cilantro
