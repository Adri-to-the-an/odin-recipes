Sushi

Description:

Sushi, a traditional Japanese dish, is made of vinegared rice and a variety of ingredients such as seafood and vegetables covered and rolled with seaweed.

This food is one of the most iconic aspects of Japanese culture and is now served up in many Japanese themed restaurants or hotels around the world. One of the best parts about sushi is its versatility as this dish can be made with a variety of ingredients to create numerous flavors and variations.

Ingredients:

  • Seaweed
  • Salmon or other fish
  • Vinegared Rice
  • Some vegetables

Steps:

  1. First spread out the seaweed on a bamboo sushi mat
  2. Add the rice onto the spread out seaweed
  3. Slice up your chosen fish and add it to the seaweed
  4. Slice up the vegetables and add to the seaweed
  5. Now roll up the seaweed using the mat and chop the roll into slices
  6. Serve and eat with sauce
